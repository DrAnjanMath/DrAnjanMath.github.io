About Me

I am an Assistant Professor (on contract) at MAKAUT, West Bengal. I completed my Ph.D. from Jadavpur University and work at the Fluvial Lab, Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata.

My research focuses on turbulence, machine learning, and computational fluid dynamics.
